About - Partners

The One D Scorecard is made possible through philanthropic support and partners in research and development. Funding was generously provided by: New Economy Initiative; The Kresge Foundation; and Bosch Community Fund. Partners in data development and research are Data Driven Detroit (D3), a Michigan Nonprofit Association Affiliate, and the Kirwan Institute for the Study of Race and Ethnicity at Ohio State University for the One D Scorecard and One Detroit Portal respectively. Development is powered by NiJeL, Inc, with design by Kat Hartman.

Please direct all questions or feedback about the One D Scorecard to Data Driven Detroit, at AskD3@datadrivendetroit.org. For questions or feedback on the Southeast Michigan Regional Opportunity Index, please contact the Kirwan Institute.
